## Chairing and other professional services

### Chairing and conference organization
- Senior Area Chair for the *Syntax: Tagging, Chunking, and Parsing* track at ACL 2023, ACL 2024, EMNLP 2025, and ACL 2026.
- Area Chair for the *Corpora and Annotation (including Tools, Systems, Treebanks)* track at LREC 2022.
- Co-Chair of the System Demo Track at EACL 2021.
- Area Chair of the Computational Social Science Track at EACL 2021.
- Co-organizer of the IWPT Shared Task on Enhanced Dependencies in 2020 and 2021.
- Co-Chair of TLT (Treebanks and Linguistic Theories) in 2020 and 2021.
- Co-Chair of SyntaxFest 2019, a joint week-long event bringing together four conferences.
- Main organizer of the SPMRL Shared Tasks in 2013 and 2014, with responsibility for dataset creation, evaluation, and the overview paper.
- General Chair of the SPMRL’09 meetup at IWPT’09, and Co-Chair of the following three workshops.

### Guest editing and reviewing
- Guest Editor of the special issue *Parsing of Morphologically Rich Languages* in *Computational Linguistics* (2013), with Reut Tsarfaty, Sandra Kübler, and Joakim Nivre.
- Since 2012, regular reviewer for the main conferences in NLP, including ACL, EMNLP, EACL, LREC, COLING, CoNLL, and TALN, as well as a number of specialized workshops such as SANCL, LAW, Disco, TLT, IWPT, Insights on Negative Results, BlackBoxNLP, and the Workshop on Noisy Text.
- Invited reviewer for the AAAI NLP track several times between 2018 and 2020.
- Reviewer for several journals, including *Computational Linguistics* (2012, 2023), *Language Resources and Evaluation* (2016, 2018), *Natural Language Engineering* (2016), *Traitement Automatique des Langues* (2011, 2020), *ACM Transactions on Asian and Low-Resource Language Information Processing* (2016, 2017), *Transactions on Affective Computing* (2024), and *Transactions of the ACL* (2025, 2026).

### Other professional services
- Board member and Information Officer of ACL SIGPARSE.
- Vice-President of the French NLP Society (ATALA) from 2018 to 2020.
- Board member of ATALA from 2009 to 2012 and again from 2017 to 2020, also serving as workshop chair during that period.
- Organizer of the Alpage team seminar series between 2012 and 2016, with Marie Candito until 2014.
- Organizer of the Almanach seminar series from its creation in 2017 until July 2021.
- Elected board member of the Département de Sociologie et Informatique at Paris-Sorbonne from 2014 to 2018, and previously of ISHA (Institut des Sciences Humaines Appliquées) from 2007 to 2011.
