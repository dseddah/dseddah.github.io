Labex EFL research operation on **deep syntax parsing**, co-led with Marie Candito and developed in collaboration with the Semagramme team in Nancy.

The goal described in the archive was to define a deep-syntax annotation scheme and associated corpora.

Main outcomes mentioned on the original page:

- **Deep Sequoia**
- **Deep FTB**

These resources were used in work around deep French parsing.
