This project on **context-enriched semantic parsing** was funded by Labex EFL.

The idea was to account for extra-linguistic context when analyzing user-generated content, by anchoring an utterance in its surrounding environment rather than treating it as an isolated text fragment.

The archived page mentions a multimodal dataset built around player interactions in *Alien vs Predator*, including discourse transcripts, non-verbal reactions, and in-game context.
